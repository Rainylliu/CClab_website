COCO is a small coffee-colored teddy dog. Because its black round eyes and nose look like three buttons, I named its “Button” in Chinese.

And the pronunciation of COCO is similar to the pronunciation of the button in Chinese. I have always dreamed of owning a puppy since I was a child, and on one of my birthdays COCO came to my home. I first met COCO when he was a little guy curled up in the corner of his cage, and I decided right then and there that I wanted to take him home.
